About Jour X

Jour X is a wardrobe for the modern wanderer. Founded in France, Jour X designs clothes for movement, transformation, and freedom.

Each garment is made-to-order to reduce waste and celebrate all body types—crafted with flowing lines, soft fabrics, and smart silhouettes that can adapt to any journey.

Our materials—cotton jersey, silk, nylon, and viscose—are chosen for their quality and their gentle impact. Whether you're chasing the sun, building your dreams remotely, or finding stillness in motion, our pieces are made to go with you, not against you.

At Jour X, every day is a fresh start. Let your wardrobe reflect that.

Founder's Message

As the Founder of Jourx, I want to grow a community of thinkers, creatives, and travelers to really remind people that its ok to live in the now. Its easy to get locked into the past or the dreams of the future but its hard to look and use what you have around you to make your day better. Life is worth all the risk, discovery, innovations, emotions (both good and bad). Using that feeling to fuel not just yourself but others is what I know that Jourx will be able to do.

Design Philosophy

At Jour X, every piece is guided by three principles:

1. Natural

We design in harmony with the body and the environment. From breathable cotton jersey to flowing silk and innovative plant-based fabrics, our materials are chosen for their softness, sustainability, and ability to adapt to real life. We let nature guide our textures, palettes, and process.

2. Modular

Your wardrobe should move with you. Our pieces are designed to be layered, styled, wrapped, or reimagined—modular clothing for modular living. Whether you're dressing for shifting climates or shifting moods, Jour X offers endless combinations with fewer, smarter garments.

3. Comfortable

Comfort is not an afterthought—it's the foundation. Every cut, drape, and seam is crafted to feel good on the skin and easy in the body. We design clothes that you can live in all day—no pinching, no stiffness, just effortless elegance and quiet confidence.
