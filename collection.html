Design Concept

Mistform represents how we move like a river. Always learning something new. No river is the same.

  • • Movement-enhancing panels and sculptural asymmetry

Look 3: Bloom

Design Concept

A draped long dress evokes organic growth, while a voluminous bomber contrasts it with strength and softness.

  •  
  • • Draped full-length dress in grey-brown viscose

Look 4: Luna

Design Concept

Luna explores the calm of lunar stillness. A transparent linen top floats over the body, cinched by a sculptural hourglass vest. The grounded base of exaggerated trousers evokes gravity, while layers remain breathable and light.

  • • Ultra-wide trousers with deep pleats
